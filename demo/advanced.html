Off-canvas menus for your website

Beautiful app look-alike off-canvas menus with sliding submenus for your website and webapp.

Try it!

Click the menu-button to open the menu on the left. Or click the contacts-button to open a second menu on the right.

Advanced

The menu on the right is really advanced; it uses the positioning and themes extensions and the counters, dragOpen, header and searchfield addons.
